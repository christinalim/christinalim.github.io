Why Collide?

Here's an example of what a typical desktop might look like on an average work day. It's disorganized, cluttered and overwhelming.

Windows are constantly being laid over each other and there are so many desktops opened in such a linear fashion.

A temporary solution to this could be reorganizing windows on the desktop or even adding external desktops, but I thought, "Why should users have to be doing this manually?"

Meet Collide

Organization.

Collide is a multi-dashboard based desktop app that allows users to categorize their applications in order to optimize productivity and eliminate distraction.

Customizable.

When curating new boards, users are given suggestions of which apps should be included based on their usage. Users can also preview what each app widget will look like before including it.

Data-driven.

Collide processes data taken from your commonly used applications to provide you with summarized and actionable visualizations.

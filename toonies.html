Toonies

Interactive.

Toonies is an interactive online learning platform designed for young adults to increase their financial literacy through easily digestible content and application of knowledge.

Users can learn about different subjects, such as investing, financial planning, and budgeting, by completing modules.

Creating profiles allows users to track their progress and stay up-to-date with new content!

Engaging.

Interactive modules are delivered in clean and clear layouts with step-by-step progressions and gamification to keep users engaged throughout the process.

Users can build simulated investment portfolios to experience varying levels of investment risk and learn more about stock tickers.

As users complete more modules, they can unlock key information on their dashboards.
